Impact on Operations

The slow and error-prone nature of manual logging can cause delays in operational activities, affecting productivity.

Safety Risks

Inaccurate or delayed logs can lead to safety hazards, as issues may not be identified and addressed in time.

Regulatory Non-Compliance

Failing to meet regulatory requirements due to inconsistent logging can result in legal and financial penalties, as well as reputational damage
